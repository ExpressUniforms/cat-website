What is Cat Lab?

A cat corner built with
curiosity and a lot of love

Cat Lab pulls fresh photos straight from The Cat API, lets you name them with a little help from Claude AI, and saves your favourites so you can revisit the ones that made you smile. There's also a whole history page — because cats have been shaping human civilisation for millennia, and they deserve credit for it.

Features

Everything you need for a
proper cat obsession

📸

Live Cat Gallery

Fresh photos pulled from The Cat API every time you visit. Twelve at a time, with a "Load More" button when you inevitably want to see another twelve.

🤖

AI Name Generator

Hit the magic wand on any photo and Claude AI analyses the cat's face and gives it a creative name with a one-sentence personality description. No two cats get the same name.

❤️

Favourites Collection

Heart any cat to save it to your personal collection. Favourites live in your browser, so they're always there waiting for you — no account needed.

🏛️

Cat History Timeline

An animated, illustrated timeline of cats through history — from the Fertile Crescent to Félicette the space cat to the golden age of cat memes.

The Gallery

New cats every visit, named by AI

Every photo is fetched live from The Cat API — a constantly-updated database of thousands of real cat photos. Tap the magic wand icon and Claude Haiku analyses the image using vision to come up with a name that actually fits the cat's vibe.
